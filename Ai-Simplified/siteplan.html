Website Planning Document

This document outlines the planning phase for the project titled "AI Simplified: A Beginner’s Guide", developed for the WDD231 course. The goal is to design a beginner-friendly educational website that introduces users to the fundamentals of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and automation.

1. Site Name

Name: AI Simplified

Reason: The name "AI Simplified" captures the purpose of the website: breaking down complex AI concepts into easy-to-understand content for complete beginners.

Suggested Domain: aisimplified.io

2. Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide an accessible introduction to Artificial Intelligence and automation for beginners. It will include explanations of key terms, practical examples, visual guides, and simple tutorials to help users understand how AI impacts daily life and how they can get started learning more.

3. Scenarios

  • "What is Artificial Intelligence and how does it work?"
  • "How can I start learning about AI without a technical background?"
  • "What are real-life examples of automation and AI being used today?"

4. Color Schema

Primary Color: #1a73e8 (used for headings, links, and call-to-action buttons)

Accent Color: #ff6f61 (used for icons, highlights, and important notes)

Primary Accent

5. Typography

Heading Font: IBM Plex Sans (used for all headings)

Body Font: Roboto (used for paragraphs and body text)

These fonts are chosen for their readability and modern design, aligning with the tech and educational theme of the website.
